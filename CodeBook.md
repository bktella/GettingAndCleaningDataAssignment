CODEBOOK.md
================

Summarizes the resulting data fields in `tidy.txt`.

## subject

integer from `1` to `30` identifying the subject of the test/training

## activity

1: `WALKING` 
2: `WALKING_UPSTAIRS` 
3: `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS` 
4: `SITTING`
5: `STANDING` 
6: `LAYING`
